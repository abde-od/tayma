Project Description

This innovative lighting design project transforms ordinary living spaces into dynamic environments through strategic use of modern LED technology and smart controls. The design emphasizes flexibility, allowing users to adjust lighting scenes based on time of day, activity, and mood.

Key features include layered lighting approach combining ambient, task, and accent lighting to create depth and visual interest. RGB LED strips provide colorful accent lighting behind furniture and architectural features, while recessed spotlights deliver focused illumination where needed.

The system integrates with smart home platforms, enabling voice control, scheduling, and automation. Preset scenes can be activated with a single command, instantly transforming the ambiance for different occasions - from bright and energizing for morning routines to warm and relaxing for evening relaxation.

Lighting Features

  • Adjustable color temperature (2700K-6500K)
  • Smart dimming for all zones
  • Circadian rhythm lighting support
  • Motion sensor integration
  • Preset mood scenes
  • Energy monitoring dashboard
