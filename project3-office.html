Project Overview

This comprehensive office space design maximizes productivity and collaboration while maintaining a modern, professional aesthetic. The open-plan layout incorporates flexible workstations, meeting rooms, and collaborative zones to support various working styles.

Strategic space planning ensures optimal natural light distribution, efficient traffic flow, and clear sight lines throughout the office. The design includes dedicated areas for focused work, team collaboration, casual meetings, and relaxation.

Electrical infrastructure was carefully planned to support modern technology needs, including power outlets at every workstation, dedicated server room circuits, and energy-efficient LED lighting systems with smart controls.

Design Goals

  • Create flexible workspace for hybrid teams
  • Maximize natural light and views
  • Optimize electrical distribution efficiency
  • Support future technology upgrades
  • Incorporate sustainable design practices
  • Balance privacy with collaboration
