System Overview

This comprehensive smart home system transforms a traditional residence into an intelligent, responsive living environment. The integration seamlessly connects lighting, climate control, security, entertainment, and energy management into a unified platform.

The system utilizes a central hub that communicates with all devices via Wi-Fi, Zigbee, and Z-Wave protocols. This multi-protocol approach ensures compatibility with a wide range of devices while maintaining reliability and responsiveness.

Voice assistants from multiple platforms are integrated, allowing residents to control their home using natural language commands. Custom automation routines adapt to lifestyle patterns, automatically adjusting settings based on time of day, occupancy, and personal preferences.
