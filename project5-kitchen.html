Project Overview

This modern kitchen design optimizes space utilization while creating an efficient and aesthetically pleasing cooking environment. The L-shaped layout maximizes counter space and provides smooth workflow between cooking zones.

Integrated appliances maintain clean lines and contemporary aesthetics. Custom cabinetry provides ample storage with soft-close mechanisms. Under-cabinet LED lighting enhances functionality and creates ambient atmosphere.

The electrical plan includes dedicated circuits for major appliances, multiple GFCI-protected outlets, and advanced ventilation systems. Smart home integration allows for remote appliance control and energy monitoring.
