Project Description

This modern bedroom design creates a serene sanctuary combining functionality with contemporary aesthetics. The space features a calming blue and gray color palette, complemented by natural wood accents and smart lighting solutions.

The design incorporates custom-built wardrobes with sleek black oval cutouts that add visual interest while providing ample storage. A dedicated study area with floating desk and modern task lighting creates a productive workspace within the bedroom.

Strategic placement of ambient and task lighting creates multiple mood settings, from bright and energizing for morning routines to soft and relaxing for evening wind-down. Smart home integration allows for voice-controlled lighting and climate management.

Design Highlights

  • Integrated smart home system with app and voice control
  • Custom moon-shaped floating shelf combining form and function
  • Multi-zone LED lighting with adjustable color temperature
  • Space-efficient wardrobe design maximizing storage capacity
